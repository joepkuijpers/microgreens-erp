# Stewardship & Governance

## Purpose

This document defines how the long-term vision, philosophy, principles, and integrity of the Microgreens ERP project are preserved.

While the software is developed as an open project, its long-term direction should remain guided by stewardship rather than ownership.

The objective is not to control who may use the project.

The objective is to ensure that the original philosophy continues to develop with integrity while welcoming responsible collaboration from individuals, organisations, researchers, and commercial partners.

## Vision

The project exists to strengthen the Food Chain for present and future generations.

Its value is not defined solely by its software.

It is equally defined by its philosophy, documentation, research, ethics, and accumulated knowledge.

Open collaboration should therefore strengthen the project rather than dilute its purpose.

Stewardship exists to preserve that purpose while allowing the project to evolve responsibly.

## Definition of Stewardship

Stewardship is the responsibility of caring for the long-term wellbeing, integrity, and direction of the project.

A steward serves the project.

The project does not serve the steward.

Stewardship is based upon responsibility, transparency, trust, and continuous learning rather than authority or ownership.

## Definition of Governance

Governance defines how important decisions are made, documented, reviewed, and communicated.

Its purpose is to ensure that significant changes remain aligned with the Vision, Mission, Core Values, Ethics, Human Flourishing Strategy, and the Growth Cycle.

Governance protects the continuity of the project while allowing thoughtful evolution.
