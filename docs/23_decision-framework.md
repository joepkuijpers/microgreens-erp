# Decision Framework

## Purpose

This document defines how decisions are made throughout the Microgreens ERP project.

Its purpose is not to prescribe individual decisions.

Its purpose is to ensure that every important decision follows a consistent, transparent, traceable, and well-understood process.

A structured decision process reduces unnecessary complexity, improves collaboration, preserves knowledge, and strengthens long-term stewardship.

---

# Philosophy

Every decision should strengthen the project.

The objective is not merely to solve today's problem.

The objective is to make decisions that continue to benefit the project as it evolves.

Good decisions arise from understanding rather than urgency.

Whenever practical, important decisions should be documented together with their reasoning.

Future contributors should understand not only what was decided, but also why it was decided.

---

# Objectives

The Decision Framework aims to:

- improve consistency;
- reduce unnecessary risk;
- support traceability;
- preserve decision history;
- strengthen collaboration;
- encourage responsible innovation;
- support long-term stewardship;
- reduce repeated discussions about previously resolved topics.

---

# Core Principles

Every important decision should have:

- a clearly defined problem;
- sufficient understanding;
- documented reasoning;
- consideration of alternatives;
- awareness of consequences;
- an accountable reviewer;
- a traceable outcome.

Decisions should remain understandable long after their implementation.
