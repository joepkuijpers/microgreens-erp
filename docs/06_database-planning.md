## Future Extensions

Future extensions should strengthen both human understanding and technological capability.

Potential developments include:

- Environmental history
- Sensor snapshots
- Historical trend analysis
- Human observations
- Human predictions
- Statistical forecasting
- Decision-support systems
- Artificial Intelligence assistance
- Automated recommendations
- Simulation models
- Knowledge graphs
- Digital twins
- Predictive maintenance
- Crop optimisation
- Sustainability indicators

The project distinguishes between:

- Observation
- Interpretation
- Prediction
- Recommendation
- Decision
- Execution

Observation may originate from people, sensors, or future technologies.

Interpretation may combine practical experience, scientific knowledge, statistical analysis, and computational methods.

Predictions may be generated by humans, mathematical models, or Artificial Intelligence.

Recommendations support decision-making but do not replace it.

Decisions remain a human responsibility unless explicitly delegated to approved automation systems.

Execution may be performed manually or through controlled automation.

This distinction preserves human understanding while enabling responsible use of advanced analytical and automation technologies.
