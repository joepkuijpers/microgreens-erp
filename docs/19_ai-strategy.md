# AI Strategy

## Purpose

This document defines the role of Artificial Intelligence within the Microgreens ERP project.

AI exists to strengthen human understanding, support responsible decision-making, and contribute to the continuous improvement of the Food Chain.

## Vision

Artificial Intelligence should extend human capability rather than replace it.

Technology should assist observation, learning, research, and decision-making while preserving human responsibility, craftsmanship, and practical knowledge.

## AI Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence is a tool.

Human understanding gives Artificial Intelligence its purpose.

Without human knowledge, experience, and observation, AI lacks the context required to support responsible decisions.

## Human Understanding Before AI

Every process should first be understood through practical experience before AI is used to support or automate it.

Understanding develops through observation, repetition, reflection, and participation.

AI should build upon this understanding rather than replace the opportunity to acquire it.

## Learning Before Automation

New AI-assisted processes should initially operate alongside independent human judgement.

Only after sufficient understanding and demonstrated reliability should greater levels of automation be considered.

Automation should be earned through understanding.

## Explainability

AI recommendations should be understandable whenever practical.

People should be able to evaluate why recommendations are made and decide whether they remain consistent with the project's principles.

## Human Validation

Periodic human validation should remain part of normal operations.

Continuous human involvement preserves practical knowledge, identifies unexpected behaviour, and ensures that AI remains aligned with biological reality.

## AI Limitations

AI cannot replace curiosity, craftsmanship, ethics, responsibility, empathy, or lived experience.

These remain uniquely human strengths.

## Guiding Principles

Technology should never become a substitute for understanding.

Understanding gives technology its purpose.

AI exists to extend human understanding, not to replace it.

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term ambition is to develop AI that strengthens research, sustainability, transparency, and responsible decision-making while continuously supporting human learning.
